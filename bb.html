Blackboard Training

Last updated: Jun 24th, 2018

Introduction

Below you will find a selection of guides designed to provide you with key skills that are required during the course development process. Carefully review each guide, as assigned by your Instructional Designer, before attending your next scheduled face-to-face or synchronous web meeting.

Course Copy

Discern how to copy a Blackboard course (1.1)

The following guide will instruct you on how to copy the contents of a current or past Blackboard course section into a new Blackboard course section. The steps in this guide were desinged to prevent common errors such as duplicating menu items or copying unwanted forum content.

Embed Media

Discover methods to embed media into Blackboard courses (1.2)

This guide will teach you how to properly embed video content in your Blackboard course. By using the university approved media platform you are less likely to violate the campus accessibility policy.

Assessments

Create, format, and publish assessments in Blackboard (1.3)

This PowerPoint presentation provides a thorough overview of all the option available to you when deploying an exam in your Blackboard course. It also covers some common points of confusion such as the differences between auto-submit and forced completion.

Download PPT

Useful Tip:

Your Instructional Designer can import test banks if you format them according to this template.

Grade Center

Become familiarized with Grade Center sorting features in Blackboard (1.4)

The Blackboard Grade Center can be customized to suit your needs. By using this guide you will learn techniques to improve readability and searchability of important content in your course's Grade Center.
