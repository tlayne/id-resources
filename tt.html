Technology Tools

Last updated: Jun 24th, 2018

Introduction

Below you will find a selection of guides related to the technology tools available at Lamar University. Carefully review each guide to become familiar with approved tools sanctioned by the university (2.4). Throughout the course development process your Instructional Designer will help you decide which tools work best for the content you are teaching.

Screen Capture

Differentiate between modes of screen capture and recording (2.1)

The University provides all faculty and students with access to Kaltura Capturespace. This tool offers a variety of recording options for capturing lectures and presentations. The following guide will instruct you on how to use each of the various modes.

Download PDF

Streaming

Recognize available options for streaming synchronous sessions (2.2)

Every Blackboard course at the university has a built in space for collaboration via the Blackboard Collaborate tool. This tool is sufficient for most classes, but if a more robust solution is needed your Instructional Designer will provide access to Adobe Connect.

Download PDF

Useful Tip:

You can further familiarize yourself with Adobe Connect using this guide.

Content Upload

Develop and upload media content such as audio and video (2.3)

Faculty and Students have access to unlimited video and audio storage using the Kaltura My Media hosting platform. This tool is built directly into Blackboard and can be accessed from the My Institution tab. This guide will teach you how to access My Media and upload content there.
