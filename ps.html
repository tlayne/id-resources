Introduction

Account

Recognize the limitations of the instructor account in Blackboard (3.1)

Some features such as self-enrollment, enrollment of teaching assistants, or the ability to merge course sections are not available under the instructor role in Blackboard. Several forms have been developed to help you overcome these limitations.
